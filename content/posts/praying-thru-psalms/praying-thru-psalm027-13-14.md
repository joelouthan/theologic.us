## The Prayer

<div style="font-variant: small-caps;">My Lord</div>

```text
I will worship you
  with all of my days.

You are my King and my God.
  and there is none like you.

You called me to wait,
  and wait upon you, I will.

Be blessed, O my Lord.
```
